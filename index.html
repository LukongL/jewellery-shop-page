About the atelier

A founder-led experience from first sketch to final polish.

Mariana built Mangata & Gallo for people who want something more intimate than a showroom and more lasting than a trend.

With a background in diamond cutting and metalsmithing, Mariana approaches every commission like a conversation: what are you celebrating, how do you want it to feel, and what details will still matter years from now?

The result is a calmer, more collaborative way to shop for jewelry, whether you are designing an engagement ring, choosing wedding bands, or marking an anniversary with a piece that feels unmistakably yours.

"The piece should feel like it already belonged to your story before you ever put it on."

Mariana, founder and lead designer

  • Private consultations for engagements, wedding sets, and milestone gifts
  • Stone sourcing guided by personal taste, timeline, and budget
  • Aftercare recommendations once the piece is officially yours

The process

A clear path from inspiration to heirloom.

The experience is intentionally simple: a focused consultation, thoughtful design direction, and finishing details you can feel every time the piece catches the light.

01

Discover

Share the occasion, your timeline, and any references so the design starts from your story.

02

Refine

03

Celebrate

Plan your piece

Tell us what you are celebrating, what silhouettes you are drawn to, and how quickly you need the piece. We will shape the next step around that, whether you are ready to design or simply narrowing your options.
